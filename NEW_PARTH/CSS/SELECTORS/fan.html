How does Fan works?

Philp Diehl invented Fan.
The principle of a fan working is based on converting electrical energy into mechanical energy. An electric motor drives the fan blades, which rotate to create airflow. The movement of the blades pushes air, creating a cooling effect as the air circulates and replaces warm air with cooler air.Fans come in various types, including ceiling fans, portable fans, and industrial fans, each with its own design and application. They can be powered by electricity, batteries, or even hand-cranks.A fan motor contains a rotating rotor and a stationary stator, both made of conductive materials, along with windings and a fan blade assembly.
