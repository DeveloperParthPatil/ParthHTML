Fingerprints are unique patterns on our fingertips that are used for identification, particularly in law enforcement and security systems. They are like a personal signature, making each person's fingerprints different, even in identical twins. This uniqueness is used to identify individuals, especially in criminal investigations and background checks.

Identification:
Fingerprints are used to identify individuals, especially in criminal investigations and background check.

Security:
Fingerprint scanners are used to verify a person's identity for access control, like unlocking a phone or entering a secure building.
